[h1]RimWorld IFTTT Automation[/h1]

Create [b]IF [Trigger] THEN [Action][/b] rules to automate your colony. Stop micromanaging — let the framework react for you.

Open the [b][IFTTT][/b] button in the bottom tab bar, write a rule, and walk away.

[hr]

[h2]Rule Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]AND / OR[/b] trigger logic with per-trigger [b]NOT[/b] inversion
[*] Ordered [b]multi-action sequences[/b] (actions fire in order)
[*] Per-rule [b]check frequency[/b] — 15 min up to 15 days (performance-friendly, not every tick)
[*] [b]Cooldown[/b] — minimum gap between firings
[*] [b]One-shot mode[/b] — auto-disables after first fire
[*] [b]Max fires cap[/b]
[*] [b]Priority system[/b] — lower number evaluated first
[*] [b]Categories[/b]: Combat / Colony / Economy / Social / Medical / Research / Alerts / Custom
[*] Live [b]fire log[/b] visible in the panel
[*] All rules saved with your game
[/list]

[hr]

[hr]

[h2]Performance[/h2]

Rules are evaluated on a per-rule configurable schedule rather than every tick. A rule set to check once per day costs essentially nothing. Even a busy ruleset with 30+ rules checking every 15 minutes is a handful of comparisons per check — no impact on frame rate.
